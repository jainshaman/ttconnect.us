Legal · privacy

Privacy policy.

Short, plain English. We collect as little as we can, we don't sell or rent your data, and we tell you when something changes.

What we collect

When you sign up for the TTConnect waitlist or newsletter, we collect:

  • Your email address — so we can email you when clubs, leagues, and events go live in your area.
  • Your ZIP code — so the email is actually useful (we send you nearby clubs, not random ones).
  • Your role (recreational player, league player, organizer) — to tailor what we send.

If you email us at ttconnectadmin@gmail.com, we also see whatever you put in the message — your name, your club, the question you asked.

What we don't collect

  • We do not collect your real name, phone number, address, or date of birth.
  • We do not run third-party analytics or advertising trackers on this site.
  • We do not set cookies for tracking. The only thing the site stores locally is a small marker if you've already submitted a form.
  • We do not record your IP address beyond standard server logs (kept 30 days, then deleted).

What we do with it

  • Send you the email(s) you signed up for — clubs near you, leagues opening, the occasional product update. Usually one email a month or less.
  • Improve the directory based on which ZIP codes are asking — e.g. if 200 people from Tampa sign up, Tampa moves up the priority list.
  • That's it. We do not sell, rent, trade, or share your email or ZIP with anyone — not clubs, not leagues, not advertisers, not "trusted partners."

How long we keep it

Your email and ZIP stay in our list until you ask us to remove them. You can ask anytime — see "Your rights" below.

Who can see it

Right now, the TTConnect team (a small handful of humans). Your data is stored with the email and form services we use to run the site — currently Google (for our admin inbox). We pick services with strong privacy track records and never use ones that re-sell personal data.

Your rights

At any time, you can email ttconnectadmin@gmail.com and ask us to:

  • Show you what we have on file about you.
  • Update your email or ZIP.
  • Delete your record entirely.
  • Stop emailing you (every email also has an unsubscribe link).

We'll do it within 7 days. No forms, no friction, no "are you sure?" guilt prompts.

Children

TTConnect is intended for adults. We don't knowingly collect information from anyone under 13. If you believe a child has signed up, email us and we'll delete the record.

Changes to this policy

If we change anything material here, we'll email everyone on the list before the change takes effect. The version date below is always current.

Contact

Questions, requests, anything at all: ttconnectadmin@gmail.com.
