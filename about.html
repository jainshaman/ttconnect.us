About TTConnect

Built by players. For players.

TTConnect is the friendly home of recreational table tennis in the United States — a free directory and community that connects leagues, clubs, and the humans who keep the rallies going.

The point of this whole thing.

Table tennis in America is everywhere and nowhere at once. There's a basement table, a community center, a clubhouse, a Tuesday night ladder — but finding them when you move to a new city, or pick the paddle up again after twenty years, can be ridiculously hard.

We started TTConnect to fix that. One website. Every club. Every league. The hours, the levels, the regulars, the vibe. The point isn't to be the official anything — it's to be the place a recreational player goes when they want to play more table tennis with more people.

We're free. We're small. We're run by people who love the game. If your league or club isn't here yet, email us — we'll add it within a day.

Our values

Four things we will not compromise on.

This is a passion project that grew up. We mean these.

01

Recreational first.

We're for the after-work crowd, the weekend warriors, the rusty-but-trying. Pros are welcome, but we will never make a beginner feel like they don't belong.

02

Free for players.

Finding a place to play should never cost money. Our directory, search, and player profiles are free forever — for everyone.

03

On the side of clubs.

Clubs run on goodwill and thin margins. We don't charge them, we don't run ads on their listings, and we don't get between them and their members.

04

Friendly, always.

The internet has enough rating arguments and forum drama. TTConnect is a place where the answer to “can I play?” is yes.
