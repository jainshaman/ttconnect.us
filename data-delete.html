Account & data deletion.

How to ask us to delete your TTConnect account, what we remove, and what we may keep — written plainly, no fine print.

TTConnect Operated by Yashwant Technologies Inc.
How to request deletion

Email us, and we'll take it from there.

To request deletion of your TTConnect account and its associated data, email us from the address linked to your account. We verify the request comes from the account owner, then process the deletion within 30 days.

Step 01 Email [email protected]

Send it from the email address associated with your account.

Step 02 Use the subject line

Set the subject to Delete My Account so we route it right.

Step 03 We verify & delete

We confirm you're the owner and complete deletion within 30 days.

Email [email protected] →

What data is deleted

When your account is deleted, we remove:

  • Your profile information — name, email address, and contact details.
  • Your player and club membership records.
  • Your match and league participation history.
  • Scores you entered.
  • Your push notification tokens.
  • Your authentication records.

What data may be retained, and for how long

A few categories may stick around after deletion, for the reasons and periods below:

What we keepWhyHow long
Records required by law Legal, tax, accounting, or fraud-prevention obligations Up to 7 years, as required by applicable law
Aggregated or anonymized data No longer identifies you Indefinitely
League & tournament results Removing them would affect the integrity of historical standings for other participants — retained in anonymized form Indefinitely, where applicable

Backups containing your data are purged on a rolling basis and fully cleared within 90 days of your deletion request.
