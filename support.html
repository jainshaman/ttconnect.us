Common questions

I forgot my password — how do I reset it?

On the sign-in screen, tap Forgot password? and enter the email address on your account. We'll send a reset link. If it doesn't arrive within a few minutes, check your spam folder or email us at [email protected].

Why is my TTConnect rating different from my USATT rating?

Your TTConnect rating reflects TTConnect league play only — it is not your official USATT rating. If you have a USATT membership number, your official status and rating are looked up separately and shown where applicable.

How do I join a league or club?

Most leagues are set up by a club or league director who adds players. If you'd like to join one near you, browse Leagues and Clubs, or email us and we'll point you to the right organizer.

A match score looks wrong. Can it be fixed?

Scores and standings are managed by your league director. Reach out to them first. If something still looks off, email [email protected] with the league name and the match in question and we'll take a look.

How do I delete my account?

Email [email protected] from your account email with the subject line "Delete My Account." We verify the request and process it within 30 days. Full details are on the account & data deletion page.
