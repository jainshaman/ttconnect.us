How TTConnect works

Three steps from couch to clubhouse.

We make it ridiculously easy to find people to play with — whether you're picking up a paddle for the first time in years or you're a seasoned league regular.

01

Find a club

Search by ZIP code or browse the map. Every listing has open-play hours, table count, fees, and what the regulars are like.

02

Join a league

Singles, doubles, ladder, round-robin — we surface leagues at your level so you're never the worst (or best) player in the room.

03

Play & connect

Track your matches, message other players, and organize sessions. The point isn't the rating — it's the rally.
